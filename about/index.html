About Me

Hi, my name is Kevin. I’m an avid road cyclist, aspiring product manager, passionate software developer, and an armchair political scientist and economist.

I study Software Engineering at the University of Waterloo and I’m currently in my 4th year. I’ve had 5 internships under my belt, with my most recent internship being at Twitch.tv in San Francisco as a software developer. I’ll be returning to Twitch this fall as a product manager intern.

This blog is where I can hopefully take a break from programming. I’ve written a ton of code in the past 4 years and haven’t done too much creative writing. You can expect posts on my cycling trips, my thoughts on world affairs and economics, and maybe a pinch of programming every so often.

Thanks for visiting!
